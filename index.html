Top three activities to do in Rwanda

BK-ARENA

East Africa's largest indoor arena in Kigali features 10,000 seats for major sports and concerts. Located next to Amahoro Stadium, it is the modern hub of Rwanda's entertainment.

Hills

Rwanda is defined by its endless rolling landscape. From the urban ridges of Kigali to the northern Virunga Volcanoes, the country offers stunning lush green views at every turn.

Mountain Gorilla

Trek through Volcanoes National Park to see endangered Mountain Gorillas in their natural habitat. These gentle giants are the highlight of Rwandan tourism and a major conservation success story.
